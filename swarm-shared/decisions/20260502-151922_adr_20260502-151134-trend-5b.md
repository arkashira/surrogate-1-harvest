## v0.1 scope
v0.1 in 2 weeks: deterministic research-to-signal workflow with Redis Streams orchestration, Postgres-backed immutable audit trail, 2 sample connectors (mock broker + mock data vendor), 3 compliance guardrails (max notional, sector denylist, velocity check), replay endpoint to re-run any workflow_run deterministically, and metrics + alerting on latency and failure rates — no production capital, fee_generating flag always false, deployable on CF Workers + Supabase free tier.

## Open questions
- Which broker/OMS connectors must be prioritized to unlock fee-generating runs in v0.1?
- What deterministic replay SLA is required (exact bitwise replay vs logical equivalence) and how will we snapshot external market data for replay fidelity?
- Compliance policy ownership: who authors and signs off guardrail rule changes, and what is the change-control cadence?
- Latency budget allocation across network, queue, and execution path to consistently achieve sub-second orchestration on free-tier infra.
- How will idempotency and exactly-once semantics be enforced across async retries and broker APIs?
