## v0.1 scope
v0.1 in 2 weeks: single workflow (research → alpha signal → risk check → simulated execution) with durable Temporal orchestration, Supabase audit trail, edge validation via Cloudflare Workers, mock broker connector, and replay CLI; includes deterministic event schema, idempotency, and basic compliance guardrails (max notional, venue allow-list).

## Open questions
- What are the hard latency budgets (p50/p99) from signal to ack across venues and which paths must be on the edge?
- Which compliance rules require synchronous block vs async escalation and what are the SLAs for human review?
- Which broker/OMS/data-vendor APIs require persistent connections (WebSocket) vs REST and how will secrets/certs be provisioned?
- How will deterministic replay be validated (event schema versioning, snapshot cadence, idempotency keys)?
- What retention and immutability guarantees (WORM, legal hold) are required for audit entries and signatures?
- Which fee-generating workflow definitions map to paid tiers and how will entitlement/metering be enforced?
