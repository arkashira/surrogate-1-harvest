## Deployment
cf workers

## v0.1 scope
v0.1 in 2 weeks: single deterministic workflow (research→signal→risk→execution) with Postgres-backed immutable audit, Redis cache for idempotency, Cloudflare Queues for dispatch, one broker connector (sandbox), one risk rule (max notional), and replay endpoint to re-run any workflow_run deterministically from stored inputs and audit log.

## Open questions
- Determinism model: do we require strict event sourcing/CQRS or is append-only audit + snapshots sufficient for replay?
- Latency SLA target for end-to-end (research→execution) on free-tier infra and acceptable p95/p99 bounds.
- Connector security posture: HSM-backed secrets vs. envelope encryption with Supabase KMS on free tier.
- Regulatory jurisdiction constraints (e.g., MiFID II best execution) and how to encode them into rule priorities.
- Backpressure and circuit-breaker policy when broker/OMS latency exceeds SLA (fail-fast vs. degrade gracefully).
