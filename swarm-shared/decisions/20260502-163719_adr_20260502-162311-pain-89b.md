## Deployment
cf workers

## v0.1 scope
v0.1 in 2 weeks: Android client (minSdk 28) with VpnService-based tunnel that wraps traffic in shadowsocks over TLS with utls Chrome fingerprint; CF Workers serve signed config + rotating front domains; local SQLite session ledger; basic kill-switch and split-tunnel allow-list; no TUN exposure (pure SOCKS5 proxy over VpnService) to avoid interface fingerprinting; WAUS/U metric stub implemented but local-only until telemetry privacy design is finalized.

## Open questions
- Which on-device port/interface hiding techniques are stable on stock Android 12+ without root (e.g., VpnService.Builder abuse, local VPN loopback, tun-fd cloaking)?
- How will we measure undetected vs detected sessions reliably without leaking telemetry that deanonymizes users?
- Which egress topology minimizes latency while maximizing plausible deniability (CF Workers tunnel + direct, or multi-hop via SF/backends)?
- How do we rotate front domains/SNI/ALPN fast enough to stay ahead of DPI updates without breaking user sessions?
- What is the acceptable tradeoff between battery use and obfuscation strength on low-end devices?
- How will we distribute and version obfuscation profiles to devices in restrictive jurisdictions without relying on app-store channels that may be blocked?
