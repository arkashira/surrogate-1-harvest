## Deployment
cf workers

## v0.1 scope
v0.1 in 2 weeks: single-asset pipeline (research payload -> signal generator -> rule-based risk check -> simulated execution) with Redis Streams orchestration, Postgres audit trail, deterministic state hash, and CF Workers API; includes replay endpoint and basic broker adapter stub (no live funds).

## Open questions
- Which brokers/data vendors must be supported in v0.1 and what auth flows do they require?
- What deterministic replay retention window and state snapshot size are acceptable for compliance?
- What hard risk limits (per trade/per day) must be enforced before execution?
- What is the maximum acceptable cold-start latency for CF Workers vs always-on container?
- How will clock synchronization and event-time ordering be guaranteed across stages?
