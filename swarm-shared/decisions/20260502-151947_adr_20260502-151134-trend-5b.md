## Deployment
cf workers

## v0.1 scope
v0.1 in 2 weeks: single deterministic workflow (research→signal→risk guardrail→execution) with Postgres (Supabase) audit trail, Cloudflare Workers queueing, one broker connector (sandbox), one risk guardrail (max-notional), replay endpoint to re-run from inputs, and basic compliance audit export (JSON).

## Open questions
- Which brokers/OMS/EMS and data vendors are must-connect for v0.1 and what auth flows do they require (OAuth2, API keys, mTLS)?
- What are the non-negotiable compliance guardrail rules (e.g., max order size, restricted symbols, time-of-day windows) and who signs off on changes?
- Deterministic replay SLA: do we need exact bitwise replay of inputs/outputs or is canonicalized JSON + schema versioning sufficient?
- Latency target for end-to-end orchestration (research+signal+risk+execution) — sub-200ms, sub-500ms, or best-effort?
- How will we store and rotate secrets (broker keys, vendor tokens) within the free-tier constraints?
- Retention and immutability requirements for audit entries (WORM, export cadence, legal hold)?
