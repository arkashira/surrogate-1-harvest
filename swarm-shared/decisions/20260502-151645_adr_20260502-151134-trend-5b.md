## v0.1 scope
v0.1 in 2 weeks: single deterministic workflow (research → signal → risk check → simulated execution) with immutable audit trail, Redis Streams queue, Postgres state, OpenTelemetry traces, and one mock broker connector; includes replay endpoint and compliance rule engine (block/warn/log) with unit/integration tests and CI on GitHub Actions.

## Open questions
- Deterministic replay SLA: do we require exact millisecond-level replay of market data ticks or is idempotent outcome replay sufficient for v0.1?
- Connector latency budgets: what are the p99/p999 targets for broker/OMS/EMS calls before we must circuit-break or fallback?
- Compliance policy DSL: should we adopt Rego/OPA or keep a minimal in-process DSL to avoid extra host/runtime on free-tier?
- Secret management on free infra: will Upstash/Supabase env-only suffice or do we need envelope encryption with sealed secrets?
- Audit retention and immutability: do we export append-only logs to Cloudflare R2 or HF datasets to ensure tamper evidence within free limits?
- Replay storage format: Avro/Parquet on R2 vs JSONL in Supabase storage for cost/performance trade-offs
