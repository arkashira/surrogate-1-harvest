## Deployment
cf workers

## v0.1 scope
v0.1 in 2 weeks: CLI-only scanner that reads AWS credentials and GCP ADC, lists Lambda + Cloud Functions schedules, persists normalized schedule records to Supabase Postgres, detects duplicates by hash and orphaned schedules by missing triggers, exports a CSV report and summary counts; no UI, no scheduler, no auto-retirement.

## Open questions
- How will we authenticate to customer AWS/GCP accounts (assume-role pattern vs. customer-supplied short-lived tokens)?
- What retention window and sampling frequency are acceptable for free-tier usage (daily vs. on-demand scans)?
- How do we define 'orphaned' across platforms (no attached trigger, no IAM permissions, or no deployed function)?
