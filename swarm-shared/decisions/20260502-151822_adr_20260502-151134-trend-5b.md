## Deployment
gcp e2-micro

## v0.1 scope
v0.1 in 2 weeks: single deterministic workflow (research->signal->risk_check->execution) with Postgres-backed immutable audit log, Redis cache/signals, one broker connector (sandbox), compliance guardrails as synchronous rule checks, idempotent run IDs, and replay endpoint to re-run from snapshot. No UI; API-first for integrators.

## Open questions
- Deterministic replay strategy: event sourcing vs snapshot+event log — which meets sub-second replay and storage cost targets on free tier?
- Idempotency and exactly-once semantics across brokers/OMS connectors — how to enforce without distributed transactions?
- Compliance guardrail DSL: declarative rules engine vs imperative hooks — which balances auditability and latency?
- Secrets/key management for broker/OMS credentials on free-tier infra (Cloudflare Workers secrets vs GCP Secret Manager free tier vs local vault)
- Latency SLA definition and measurement — target p95 end-to-end orchestration latency and acceptable variance across venues
