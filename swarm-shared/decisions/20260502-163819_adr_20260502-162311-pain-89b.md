## Deployment
cf workers

## v0.1 scope
v0.1 in 2 weeks: Android app (minSdk 24) that creates an obfuscated SOCKS5 listener and TUN via VpnService; Noise IK handshake to a Rust edge worker on CF that forwards traffic; config and blocklist distributed via Workers KV; local SQLite for ephemeral state; no accounts required (device key only); basic reachability and throughput tests; minimal telemetry (session duration, bytes, detected flag) hashed per device; documentation for sideload and reproducible builds.

## Open questions
- Which traffic obfuscation primitives minimize DPI fingerprints while preserving throughput on high-loss paths (steg vs port-hopping vs domain-fronting via CF Workers)?
- How will we distribute and rotate blocklists and endpoint hints without leaking metadata to on-path observers (CF KV vs direct Supabase vs Bloom filters)?
- What is the acceptable tradeoff between TUN MTU and packet overhead to avoid size-based fingerprinting while keeping latency low?
- How do we detect and respond to on-device VPN-detection heuristics (e.g., /proc/net/tun visibility, UID routing rules) on stock vs hardened Android?
- What telemetry can we safely collect to measure WAUS/U without deanonymizing users or creating honeypot logs?
