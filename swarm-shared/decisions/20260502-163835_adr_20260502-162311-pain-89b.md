## v0.1 scope
v0.1 ships in 2 weeks: Android app (no root) that establishes a local shadowsocks-rust SOCKS5 proxy, uses tun-based routing via Android VPNService, fetches per-device config bundles from Supabase through a Cloudflare Worker, applies port masquerade and domain-fronting cover traffic to hide proxy/TUN fingerprints, and records local session/obfs_event logs (no PII) to measure undetected sessions; free-tier deployable backend (Worker + Supabase) with zero-cost OSS dependencies.

## Open questions
- Which cover protocols and domain-fronting hosts minimize collateral blocking while maximizing reach in target jurisdictions?
- How will we rotate ephemeral SOCKS5 ports and TUN interface naming to avoid static fingerprints without breaking app connectivity?
- What local heuristics will detect active network inspection (DPI, SNI/ALPN probing) and trigger obfuscation mode?
- How do we balance kernel bypass (raw sockets/TUN) with stock Android VPNService constraints to remain installable without root/MDM?
- What telemetry (if any) can be collected without deanonymizing users to measure WAUS/U and detect blocking events?
