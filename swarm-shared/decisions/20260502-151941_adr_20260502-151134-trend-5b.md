## Deployment
cf workers

## v0.1 scope
v0.1 in 2 weeks: one deterministic workflow (research→signal→risk→execution) with Postgres-backed state and Redis cache, idempotent run/retry, immutable step snapshots, one broker connector (sandbox REST), one compliance guardrail (max notional per run), audit export (JSONL), and CI tests; deployable on CF Workers + Supabase free tier.

## Open questions

- What deterministic replay SLA is required (exact bitwise vs logical equivalence) and how long must inputs/snapshots be retained?
- What compliance guardrail primitives are non-negotiable for launch (e.g., per-instrument position limits, kill-switch latency thresholds)?
- How will secrets/credentials be managed on free-tier infra (CF Workers KV, Supabase Vault, or local encrypted env)?
- What latency budget per stage (research→signal→risk→execution) is acceptable to count as 'sub-second' for NSM?
