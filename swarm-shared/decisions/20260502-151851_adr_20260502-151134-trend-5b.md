## Deployment
cf workers

## v0.1 scope
v0.1 in 2 weeks: single deterministic workflow (research→signal→risk→execution) with Postgres-backed immutable audit, Redis cache for risk lookups, Cloudflare Queues for dispatch, one broker connector (sandbox), one OMS connector (sandbox), and compliance guardrails that block on hard rules and log warnings; includes replay endpoint to re-run any run id deterministically and NSM telemetry for monthly successful workflow runs.

## Open questions
- Determinism boundaries: which workflow stages must be pure functions to guarantee replayability?
- Latency SLO per stage (research→signal→risk→execution) to validate CF Workers vs HF Space choice.
- Retention and WORM requirements for audit entries (legal hold vs TTL).
- Secrets management strategy on free-tier infra (CF Workers Secrets vs Supabase encrypted columns).
- Connector idempotency and at-least-once vs exactly-once semantics for broker/OMS/EMS APIs.
