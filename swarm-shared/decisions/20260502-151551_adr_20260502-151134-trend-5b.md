## Deployment
cf workers

## v0.1 scope
v0.1 in 2 weeks: deterministic workflow engine on CF Workers + Postgres (Supabase) with Redis cache; one pre-built broker connector (sandbox), one signal adapter, three compliance guardrails (venue, size, time-of-day), immutable audit log, replay endpoint for runs, and NSM metric export (Prometheus). No frontend; API-first for integrators.

## Open questions
- Which brokers/OMS/EMS and data vendors are must-connect for v0.1 and what auth flows do they require (OAuth, API keys, mTLS)?
- What are the non-negotiable compliance guardrail rules (venue/venue-time restrictions, order size limits, pre-trade checks) and who owns rule changes?
- Deterministic replay requirements: must we snapshot full market data or only inputs/signals? What retention period for immutable audit logs?
- Latency SLO for end-to-end orchestration (research→signal→risk→execution) and acceptable p95/p99 on free-tier infra?
- How will NSM attribution work (weighted by fee-generating status) and how do we surface it for ops review?
- Disaster recovery and backtesting replay strategy: can we replay via CF Workers + Postgres or do we need separate compute?
