## v0.1 scope
v0.1 in 2 weeks: single deterministic workflow (research→signal→risk check→execution stub) with Postgres-backed immutable audit trail, Redis-backed queue and cache, idempotency keys, OpenTelemetry traces, and a CF Worker endpoint to submit/query runs; includes one mock broker connector and one compliance guardrail (max position limit) with pass/fail outcomes and replayable run history.

## Open questions
- What are the deterministic replay requirements (exact byte-for-byte vs logical equivalence) and retention window for audit?
- Which broker/OMS/EMS and data-vendor APIs are in scope for v0.1 connectors and what auth schemes do they require (OAuth2, API keys, mTLS)?
- What compliance rule latency budget is acceptable (sub-100ms vs sub-500ms) and who owns rule updates/versioning?
- How should partial failures be handled (compensating actions, sagas) and what rollback semantics are required?
- What are the rate limits and circuit-breaker thresholds for external execution APIs to avoid breaches or throttling?
