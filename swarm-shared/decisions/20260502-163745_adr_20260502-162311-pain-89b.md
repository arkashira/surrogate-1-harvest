## v0.1 scope
v0.1 ships in 2 weeks: Android app (Kotlin + Rust JNI) with tun-hidden mode (boringtun in userspace), shadowsocks+Obfs4 obfuscated proxy, ephemeral SOCKS5-over-TLS on port 443 cover, local SQLite config, Supabase Postgres for opt-in anonymous session telemetry, and a Cloudflare Worker for endpoint health checks and feature flags; no account required, on-device keygen, and basic WAUS/U counter per-install.

## Open questions
- Which obfuscation primitives (shadowsocks+Obfs4 vs. FTE vs. domain-fronting) maximize undetectability on Russian DPI while staying performant on mobile?
- How will we measure WAUS/U reliably without leaking telemetry that deanonymizes users?
- Can we mask TUN interfaces on stock Android without root while preserving throughput and battery life?
- What is our threat model for endpoint discovery and revocation under active probing?
- How do we handle captive portals and carrier-grade NAT without exposing SOCKS5 ports?
- Which local storage strategy (encrypted Room vs. in-memory) balances resilience against device seizure and deniability?
