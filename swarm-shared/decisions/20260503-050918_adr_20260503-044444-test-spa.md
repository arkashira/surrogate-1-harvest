## Deployment
cf workers

## v0.1 scope
v0.1 in 2 weeks: CLI-first MVP that connects to one AWS account and one GCP project, enumerates schedules, stores them in Supabase Postgres, detects duplicates (identical cron+target), drift (cron changed since last run), and orphans (function missing), exposes a minimal FastAPI endpoint to list findings and mark schedules retired; no queue/cache, no frontend, deployable as a CF Worker or local CLI.

## Open questions
- Which IAM/service-account scopes are acceptable for read-only schedule enumeration on customer Lambda + Cloud Functions?
- How will customers grant access (assume cross-account roles vs. customer-provided tokens)?
- What defines 'orphaned' in practice (no attached function, or no recent invocations + no triggers)?
- Retention policy for raw metadata vs. summarized audit history to stay within Supabase free tier
