## Deployment

## v0.1 scope
v0.1 in 2 weeks: one strategy (single symbol), research→signal→risk→execution pipeline with deterministic run_id, Redis Streams for queueing, Supabase Postgres for durable state and audit events, mock broker adapter (simulated fills), risk guardrails (static per-trade and per-day limits), replay CLI to reprocess runs from events, and perf harness to measure median signal-to-execution latency <500ms on free-tier infra.

## Open questions
- Which brokers/data vendors must be supported in v0.1 and what auth flows do they require (API keys/oauth)?
- What are the hard risk limits (per-symbol, portfolio, margin) and who configures them?
- Deterministic replay requirements: do we need event sourcing with snapshots or append-only event log?
- Latency SLO measurement methodology: clock sync (NTP/PTP) between signal, risk, and execution hops?
- Regulatory/compliance retention: how long must audit events be retained and where (Supabase or cold storage)?
- Failover and idempotency: how to handle duplicate broker executions and at-least-once queue semantics?
