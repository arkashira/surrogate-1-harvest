## v0.1 scope
v0.1 in 2 weeks: single deterministic workflow (research→signal→risk→execution stub) with immutable audit trail, replay capability, one broker connector mock, Redis Streams queue, Postgres event store, and edge worker endpoints; supports manual trigger and one compliance rule set.

## Open questions
- Deterministic replay boundary: should replay re-evaluate risk rules or freeze them as-of original run?
- Broker/OMS connector auth model: OAuth2 device flow vs signed JWTs for server-to-server?
- Latency SLA target for end-to-end (research→execution) on free-tier infra?
- Retention and immutability policy for audit logs (CF R2 vs Supabase storage) and cost envelope?
- How to version workflow definitions and step schemas without breaking deterministic replay?
- Compliance rule hot-reload semantics and rollback strategy during incidents?
