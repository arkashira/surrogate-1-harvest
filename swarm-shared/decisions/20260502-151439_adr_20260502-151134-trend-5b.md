## Deployment
cf workers

## v0.1 scope
v0.1 in 2 weeks: deterministic workflow engine (DAG runner) on CF Workers with Postgres-backed queue (pg-boss) and immutable audit trail; one research connector, one signal connector, one risk-check guardrail (max position size), and one simulated execution connector; replay CLI to re-run any workflow_run deterministically; latency metrics per step and basic NSM counter (monthly successful workflow runs weighted by fee_generating).
