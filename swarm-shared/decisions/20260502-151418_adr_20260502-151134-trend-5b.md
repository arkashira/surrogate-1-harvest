## Deployment
cf workers

## v0.1 scope
v0.1 in 2 weeks: single deterministic workflow (research->signal->risk->execution) with immutable audit trail on Supabase, Cloudflare Worker API + Redis Streams queue, one broker connector (sandbox), one risk guardrail (max position size), replay endpoint that re-runs from inputs_hash, and basic compliance violation logging. No frontend; CLI and API only.

## Open questions
- Deterministic replay fidelity: which inputs and environment metadata must be snapshotted to guarantee bitwise replay across infra resets?
- Latency SLA per step: what are hard ceilings (e.g., risk<5ms, queue<20ms) and acceptable tail percentiles for production?
- Compliance rule engine scope: will rules be expressed as code, declarative JSON, or a hybrid DSL, and who owns versioning?
- Connector ownership model: who rotates secrets and how are credential changes propagated without downtime?
- Failure modes and compensations: what automated rollback/hedge actions are allowed when execution step partially fails?
- Audit retention and immutability guarantees: WORM storage strategy and legal hold requirements for regulated jurisdictions?
