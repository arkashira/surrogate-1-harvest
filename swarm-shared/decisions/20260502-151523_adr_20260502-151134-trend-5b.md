## Deployment
cf workers

## v0.1 scope
v0.1 (2 weeks): single-instrument (e.g., BTC/USD) pipeline with research (public OHLCV fetch), signal (threshold rule), risk (per-trade cap + hard stop), execution (paper/sandbox broker via REST), deterministic Redis Stream pipeline, Postgres audit log, and CF Worker endpoints for submit/status/replay. Target: ≥1 live pipeline run/day with median signal-to-execution <500ms and full audit trail.

## Open questions
- Which brokers/data vendors must be supported in v0.1 and what auth flows do they require (OAuth2, API keys, JWT)?
- What is the deterministic replay strategy for Redis Streams (snapshot + event log vs changelog) and retention window?
- What are the hard risk guardrails (max position size, per-instrument limits, circuit breakers) and who configures them?
- Target latency SLO per stage (research→signal, signal→risk, risk→execution) to meet <500ms median end-to-end?
- How will clock sync and timestamp precision be handled across brokers and internal pipeline (NTP/PTP strategy)?
- Compliance requirements for audit retention (immutable log storage, WORM) and where will long-term logs live?
- Failure modes and retry/backoff policy for broker timeouts and partial fills (idempotency key scheme)?
- Will HF Spaces be used for model hosting or research inference, and what latency/cost trade-offs apply?
