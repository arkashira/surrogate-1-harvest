## Deployment
cf workers

## v0.1 scope
v0.1 in 2 weeks: CLI-only scanner that connects to one AWS account and one GCP project, lists EventBridge rules + Cloud Scheduler jobs, maps targets to Lambda/Cloud Functions, persists schedules and detected issues (duplicate/disabled/orphaned) to Supabase Postgres, and outputs a summary report with suggested retirements.

## Open questions
- Which identity will the auditor use to list Lambda schedules and Cloud Functions across customer accounts (assume cross-account IAM roles or service principals)?
- How will we detect 'drifted' schedules (code/config mismatch) without invoking functions — diff metadata vs. infra-as-code or tags?
- Retention and GDPR: how long to keep raw cloud metadata in Postgres and who can delete it?
- Will scans be pull-based (scheduled CF worker) or push-based (customer webhook/CLI upload) to avoid long-running cloud credentials on free tiers?
