## Deployment
cf workers

## v0.1 scope
v0.1 in 2 weeks: CLI + FastAPI service deployable on CF Workers (or local dev) that connects to Supabase Postgres; reads Lambda schedules (boto3) and Cloud Scheduler jobs (GCP); detects orphaned (missing target), drifted (cron changed), and duplicate (same cron+target+env) schedules; writes results to schedule + audit_run tables; exposes GET /audit/run (trigger) and GET /schedules?state=... (list); no frontend, no queue, no cache; open-source deps only; free-tier-first.
