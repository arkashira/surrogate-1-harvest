## v0.1 scope
v0.1 ships a deterministic, auditable research→signal→risk→execution workflow runner on HF Space (FastAPI) with Supastore-backed immutable audit trails, Redis-backed sub-ms guardrail checks, Cloudflare Queues for retries, and pre-built mock connectors for one broker + one data vendor; includes compliance guardrails (pre-trade limit checks, blacklist, order-size caps) and replayable runs with content-addressed hashes.

## Open questions
- Deterministic replay requirements: must inputs+guardrail decisions be content-addressed and signed for audit, or is DB-level immutability sufficient for v0.1?
- Latency SLA for sub-second orchestration: target p95 end-to-end (research→execution) on free-tier infra?
- Compliance scope for v0.1: which jurisdictions/rules must guardrails enforce (e.g., pre-trade limit checks, blacklists, order-size caps)?
- Connector strategy: will broker/OMS/EMS integrations use webhooks, REST polling, or FIX; and which adapters are in scope for v0.1?
- Idempotency and exactly-once semantics: can HF Space + Cloudflare Queues provide at-least-once with idempotency keys, or do we need stronger coordination?
- Data retention and PII: what research/signal fields require encryption or redaction for regulatory reasons?
