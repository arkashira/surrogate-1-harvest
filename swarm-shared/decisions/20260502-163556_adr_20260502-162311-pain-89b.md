## Deployment
cf workers

## v0.1 scope
v0.1 in 2 weeks: Android app (no root) that creates a WireGuard-like secure tunnel via boringtun, exposes a local SOCKS5 listener, and wraps traffic through a meek HTTP/S WebSocket fronted by a Cloudflare Worker; endpoint selection from Supabase-hosted config; short-lived capability tokens; basic health checks and local SQLite state; free-tier hosting on CF Workers + Supabase; no custom obfuscation beyond meek + profile rotation; target: establish undetected sessions on stock Android under basic DPI.

## Open questions
- Which pluggable transports (snowflake/meek/other) are least likely to be blocked by current DPI in target jurisdictions?
- Can we reliably hide TUN interfaces on stock Android without root while preserving throughput (boringtun + VpnService tun fd strategy)?
- What is the acceptable trade-off between battery usage and stealth keepalive cadence under active probing?
- How will we distribute endpoint/obfs-profile updates to offline-first devices (Workers KV + periodic fetch vs. push)?
- What telemetry (if any) is permissible under privacy constraints while still measuring WAUS/U?
