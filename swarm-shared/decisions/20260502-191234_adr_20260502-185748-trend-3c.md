## v0.1 scope
CLI-only v0.1 in 2 weeks: local repo indexing, Python/JS/TS parsing via tree-sitter, rule-based findings (ruff + custom rules), severity tagging, JSON/terminal output, SQLite storage, and optional push of anonymized summary to HF Space for shared benchmarks (opt-in).

## Open questions
- Will large repos require chunking strategies to fit into local memory during analysis?
- Which subset of languages must v0.1 support to deliver immediate value?
- Should team/shared metadata use Supabase Postgres or remain purely local?
- What is the acceptable trade-off between local model size and accuracy for semantic findings?
- How will licensing and distribution of models comply with HF and OSS licenses?
