## v0.1 scope
v0.1 in 2 weeks: single deterministic workflow (research → signal → risk check → compliance guardrail → simulated execution stub) with idempotent Redis Streams event log, Postgres-backed immutable audit_event, risk limit cache in Redis, and replay endpoint; includes one sample broker/OMS connector mock, OpenTelemetry traces, and contract tests for workflow determinism and latency under 500ms for happy path.

## Open questions
- What deterministic workflow engine will we use (Temporal OSS vs custom Redis Streams + saga) to guarantee replayability and exactly-once semantics on free-tier infra?
- Which brokers/OMS/EMS are must-have connectors for v0.1 and what auth/credential storage model satisfies compliance on Cloudflare Workers?
- What is the maximum acceptable end-to-end latency SLA (ms) for sub-second orchestration given CF Workers + Postgres + Redis latencies?
- How will we implement tamper-evident audit trails (hash-chaining/WAL export) without managed ledger services?
- What retention and redaction policy applies to PII/trade data in audit_event to satisfy regulatory constraints on free-tier storage?
