## v0.1 scope
v0.1 ships in 2 weeks: single deterministic workflow (research->signal->risk->execution) with one broker connector, Postgres-backed immutable audit log, Redis-backed signal/risk cache, idempotent API, replay endpoint for runs, and compliance guardrails (hard limits + rule engine) — deployable on Cloudflare Workers + Supabase free tier.

## Open questions
- Deterministic replay: should event sourcing be append-only (event store) or snapshot-based (state+log) to bound storage on free tier?
- Latency SLA: what is the p99 target for research->execution to qualify as sub-second orchestration?
- Compliance scope: which jurisdictions/rulesets (e.g., MiFID II, SEC 15c3-5) must guardrails enforce in v0.1?
- Idempotency and exactly-once: will brokers/OMS support idempotency keys or do we need compensating transactions?
- Secrets and key management: how to store broker/OMS credentials on free-tier edge/runtime without persistent secrets stores?
- Connector ownership: who maintains pre-built connectors (broker/OMS/EMS) and what is the SLA for API changes?
- Replay fidelity: do we need wall-clock synchronization (NTP) or logical clocks to ensure deterministic replay across regions?
