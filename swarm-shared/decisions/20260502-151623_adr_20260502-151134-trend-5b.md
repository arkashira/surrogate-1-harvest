## Deployment
cf workers

## v0.1 scope
v0.1 in 2 weeks: deterministic workflow engine (research→signal→risk→execution stub) with Postgres-backed immutable audit trail, one sample broker connector (sandbox), one compliance guardrail (max position size), replay endpoint for a single workflow definition, and CI that runs deterministic replay tests; deployed on CF Workers with Supabase Postgres + Redis (free tiers).

## Open questions
- What deterministic replay SLA do we need (exact input snapshot vs. idempotency keys) and how will we version connector contracts?
- Which brokers/OMS/EMS and data vendors are must-have connectors for v0.1 and what auth mechanisms do they require?
- What compliance rule DSL and change-control process will satisfy audit requirements without blocking deploys?
- What is the maximum acceptable end-to-end latency (ms) and how will we measure and enforce it under load?
- How will secrets/keys be rotated and stored (CF KV + Supabase Vault or external OSS secret store) while staying free-tier friendly?
