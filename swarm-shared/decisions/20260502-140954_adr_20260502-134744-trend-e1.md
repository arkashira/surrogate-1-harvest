## v0.1 scope
v0.1 in 2 weeks: headless API (FastAPI) on CF Workers (or HF Space) with Supabase Postgres + Redis; identity CRUD, alias ingestion endpoint, simple deterministic attribution engine (exact-match on service+external_id), Redis Streams job queue, basic incident CRUD and linking, Swagger docs, unit/integration tests, and docker-compose local dev — no frontend, no ML, no third-party paid services.

## Open questions
- Which external services/signals will be integrated first (e.g., email, phone, device, IP) and what auth/API limits apply?
- What confidence threshold and deterministic vs. probabilistic rules will drive automatic merges vs. human review?
- Data residency and retention policy: how long to retain raw signals and alias mappings?
- How will false-positive merges be surfaced and rolled back by trust and safety operators?
- Will real-time vs. batch attribution be required for v0.1 and what latency SLA is acceptable?
