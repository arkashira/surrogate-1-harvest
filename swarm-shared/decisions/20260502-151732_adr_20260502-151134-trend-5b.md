## v0.1 scope
v0.1 in 2 weeks: deterministic workflow engine (research→signal→risk→execution) with Postgres-backed immutable audit log, Redis Streams for orchestration, FastAPI endpoints to submit/query runs, two reference connectors (one mock broker + one data vendor), compliance guardrails (hard limits, allowlists, risk checks) and replay endpoint for audit; deployed on Cloudflare Workers + Supabase free tier + Redis OSS on free runner; no UI, API-first for integrators.

## Open questions
- Which broker/OMS/EMS connectors must be prioritized to unlock fee-generating runs in v0.1?
- What deterministic replay SLA is required (exact bitwise replay vs logical equivalence) and how will we sign/store audit events to satisfy compliance?
- Latency budget per stage (research→signal→risk→execution) to meet sub-second orchestration on free-tier infra?
- Retention and encryption posture for PII/trade data under regulatory regimes we must support at launch?
- How will we implement idempotency and exactly-once semantics across Redis Streams and Postgres under concurrent replays?
