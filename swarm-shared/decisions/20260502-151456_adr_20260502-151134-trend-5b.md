## v0.1 scope
v0.1 in 2 weeks: single deterministic workflow (research→signal→risk guardrail→execution stub) with Postgres audit trail, Cloudflare Queue dispatch, Redis cache, and replay CLI; includes one mock broker connector and one real broker sandbox (e.g., Alpaca paper) to prove sub-second latency and immutable audit.

## Open questions
- Deterministic replay: should inputs_hash include normalized clock/reference-time or rely on idempotency keys only?
- Edge vs origin: keep all queue+cache on CF Workers or use HF Space Redis for cross-region resilience?
- Signature key management for immutable_log: use per-tenant ephemeral keys or a single service key with rotation?
- Broker/OMS connector SLAs: what is acceptable retry/backoff to preserve sub-second latency?
- Compliance retention: how long must immutable_log be queryable vs archived to cold storage?
