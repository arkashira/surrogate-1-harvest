## v0.1 scope
v0.1 in 2 weeks: deterministic research→signal→risk→execution workflow runner (CF Workers), immutable audit trail in Supabase Postgres, Redis-backed hot signal/risk state, pre-built mock connectors for one broker and one data vendor, compliance guardrails as hard-stop rules with pass/fail audit entries, and replay endpoint to re-run a workflow run deterministically from stored inputs/audit.

## Open questions
- What are the hard compliance guardrail SLAs (max decision latency and determinism requirements) for production?
- Which brokers/OMS/EMS and data vendors must be supported in v0.1 and what auth/credential model will be used?
- How will replay/rewind be triggered (time-travel queries vs event sourcing) and what retention window is required for audit?
- What is the acceptable staleness window for signals and risk limits before a run is blocked/quarantined?
- Will CF Workers KV/Durable Objects suffice for state vs requiring Redis, and what is the failover plan?
