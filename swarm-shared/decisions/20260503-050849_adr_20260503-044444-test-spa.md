## v0.1 scope
v0.1 in 2 weeks: CLI-first MVP that connects to one AWS account + one GCP project, lists all schedules, flags orphans (function missing) and duplicates (same cron+target hash), exports CSV/JSON report, and persists findings to Supabase Postgres with basic REST endpoints for CRUD on schedule metadata.
