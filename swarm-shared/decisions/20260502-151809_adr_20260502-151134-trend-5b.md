## Deployment
cf workers

## v0.1 scope
v0.1 in 2 weeks: single deterministic workflow (research/signal → risk check → execution) with one broker connector (sandbox), Redis-backed queue/cache, Postgres (Supabase) for definitions/runs/executions/audit, immutable audit events, idempotent runs, and basic compliance guardrails (max notional, per-symbol limits) exposed via FastAPI; no UI, deployable on CF Workers + Supabase free tier.

## Open questions
- Deterministic replay: should checkpoints be event-sourced (append-only) or state-snapshot based?
- Latency SLA for sub-second orchestration: target p95 end-to-end (research→execution) under real market feed?
- Compliance rule engine: DSL vs. inline Python predicates; who authors/updates rules and approval workflow?
- Connector reliability model: retries, circuit breakers, and idempotency key strategy per broker/OMS?
- Secrets/credentials model for broker/OMS keys: CF Workers Secrets, Supabase Vault, or external KMS (free-tier constraints)?
- Audit retention and immutability: WORM storage or signed append-only log; retention period for regulatory checks?
