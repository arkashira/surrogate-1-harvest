## Deployment
cf workers (free-tier) + Supabase (free-tier Postgres) + optional HF Space for Redis-backed workers

## v0.1 scope
v0.1 in 2 weeks: single deterministic workflow (research→signal→risk→execution) with Postgres audit trail, Redis-backed risk limit checks, one broker connector (sandbox), replay endpoint for runs, and CF Workers + Supabase free-tier deployment; no UI, API-first.

## Open questions
- Deterministic replay fidelity: should we snapshot full market data snapshots or rely on idempotent inputs + clock-sync?
- Latency SLA target for end-to-end orchestration (sub-500ms vs sub-100ms) and acceptable queueing semantics
- Compliance rule engine: DSL vs code-first policies and hot-reload requirements
- Broker/OMS connector authentication model (OAuth2 client-credentials vs signed JWT) and secret rotation cadence
- Retention and storage costs for immutable audit entries (WAL vs cold storage) under free-tier constraints
