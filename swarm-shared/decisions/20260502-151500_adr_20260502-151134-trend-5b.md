## Deployment
cf workers

## v0.1 scope
v0.1 in 2 weeks: single long-only workflow (research → signal → risk → simulated execution) with deterministic Redis-stream pipeline, Postgres audit trail, one broker connector (paper/sandbox), one data vendor mock, risk rule (max position size), replay CLI, and perf tests proving median signal-to-execution <500ms on free-tier infra.

## Open questions
- Which brokers/data vendors must be supported in v0.1 and what auth flows (API keys/oauth) do they require?
- What deterministic replay granularity is required for compliance (event-level vs state snapshot) and retention period?
- What hard risk guardrails are non-negotiable (max notional, per-instrument caps, kill-switch latency)?
- Can we tolerate at-most-once vs exactly-once execution semantics given broker idempotency guarantees?
- Where will Redis run (CF KV-like offering, Supabase Redis, or external free-tier) to meet <500ms p95 end-to-end?
