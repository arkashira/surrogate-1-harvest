## v0.1 scope
v0.1 in 2 weeks: single deterministic workflow (research→signal→risk check→execution) with one broker connector, immutable Postgres audit trail via Supabase, CF Workers queue orchestration, Redis-backed risk cache, idempotency keys, and replay endpoint for runs; no UI, API-only, free-tier infra only.

## Open questions
- Deterministic replay: which inputs and external state snapshots are required to replay a workflow run exactly?
- Idempotency strategy: how do we deduplicate across broker/OMS retries and CF Workers at-most-once delivery?
- Compliance rule engine: DSL vs. code-first policies; who authors and version-controls rules?
- Latency SLOs: target p95 end-to-end from signal to execution under normal market conditions?
- Connector auth model: per-client secrets in CF Workers KV vs. Supabase storage; rotation cadence?
- Audit retention and export: immutable retention period and format for regulatory requests?
