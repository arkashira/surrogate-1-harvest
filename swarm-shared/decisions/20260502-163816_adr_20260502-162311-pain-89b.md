## v0.1 scope
v0.1 ships in 2 weeks: Android app (minSdk 26) with tun concealment, Shadowsocks-like obfuscated tunnel over TLS+ALPN to Cloudflare Workers edge egress, OTA obfuscation profiles via Supabase + CF Workers, local encrypted SQLite policy, and basic WAUS/U telemetry hashed/anonymized; no root or Knox required, works on stock hardened Android.

## Open questions
- Which obfuscation primitives balance detectability vs throughput on restrictive Middleboxes (MTU/probing behavior)?
- Can we safely hide TUN interface names and SOCKS5 listeners from local apps without root or Knox?
- What is the acceptable false-positive rate for auto-triggering obfuscation when scans are detected?
- How will we distribute and rotate fronting domains/CDN configs without centralized takedown risk?
- What telemetry (if any) can be collected without deanonymizing users or leaking presence?
