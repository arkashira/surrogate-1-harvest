## v0.1 scope
v0.1 in 2 weeks: single deterministic workflow (research->signal->risk->execution) with Postgres-backed immutable audit trail, Cloudflare Queues for replayable task orchestration, Redis-backed risk/signal cache, and pre-built mock connectors for one broker and one data vendor; includes compliance guardrails (max position, blacklist) and NSM telemetry for monthly successful fee-weighted workflow runs.
