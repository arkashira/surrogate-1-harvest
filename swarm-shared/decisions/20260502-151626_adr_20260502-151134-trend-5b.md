## v0.1 scope
v0.1 in 2 weeks: deterministic workflow engine (research→signal→risk→execution) with Redis Streams replay, Postgres audit trail (Supabase), one broker connector (paper/sandbox) and one data vendor connector, risk guardrails (hard block on per-trade notional limit), edge-to-edge latency measurement, and CLI replay for audits — no frontend, API-first.

## Open questions
- What deterministic event schema and versioning strategy will guarantee replay equivalence across connector updates?
- Which compliance guardrails are non-negotiable for launch (e.g., hard block vs. soft warn) and how are they sourced (static rules vs. external policy service)?
- What latency SLA (p50/p95) from signal to execution is required to preserve edge, and which connectors must meet it?
- How will secrets/encrypted connector credentials be rotated and audited on free-tier infra (Supabase + Workers + Upstash)?
- What is the minimal viable audit retention period and export format to satisfy regulatory checks while staying on free tiers?
