## v0.1 scope
v0.1 in 2 weeks: deterministic research→signal→risk→execution workflow runner with Redis-backed queue, Postgres (Supabase) persistence, immutable audit log, one broker connector (REST), one risk guardrail (max notional), replay endpoint to re-run a run by payload_hash, and NSM tracking for monthly successful fee-generating workflow runs.

## Open questions
- Which broker/OMS/EMS connectors must be prioritized for v0.1 (FIX, REST, WebSocket)?
- What is the deterministic replay SLA (exact byte-for-byte vs logical equivalence) and retention window?
- Compliance rule authoring: DSL vs JSON schema vs code — who authors and how are changes promoted?
- Latency budget per hop (research→signal→risk→execution) and acceptable tail percentiles (p95/p99)?
- Idempotency and deduplication strategy across connectors (idempotency keys vs exactly-once semantics)?
- Secrets/credentials management for connectors on free-tier infra (CF Workers secrets vs Supabase edge functions)?
