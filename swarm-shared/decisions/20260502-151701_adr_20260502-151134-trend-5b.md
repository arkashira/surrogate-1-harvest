## v0.1 scope
v0.1 in 2 weeks: deterministic workflow engine with research→signal→risk→execution path, Postgres-backed immutable audit log, Redis queue/cache, one broker connector (sandbox), one risk guardrail (max notional), replay endpoint for runs, and NSM telemetry (successful workflow runs weighted by fee-generating status).

- How will secrets/credentials for connectors be provisioned and rotated on free-tier infra?
