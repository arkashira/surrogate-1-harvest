## v0.1 scope
v0.1 in 2 weeks: single deterministic workflow (research signal -> risk check -> single-leg execution stub) with immutable audit trail, Redis-backed queue and snapshot cache, Postgres (Supabase) persistence, Cloudflare Worker trigger, mock broker/OMS adapter, compliance guardrail DSL (allow/block), replay harness for workflow runs, and NSM telemetry (weighted successful runs).

## Open questions
- Deterministic replay fidelity: which state must be snapshotted (order book, positions, risk ledger) to guarantee bitwise replay?
- Idempotency and exactly-once semantics across brokers/OMS: how to reconcile at-least-once delivery with compliance audit requirements?
- Latency SLOs for sub-second orchestration: target p99 end-to-end and acceptable queueing budget per stage?
- Compliance rule evaluation engine: DSL vs. inline Python, and how to version/rollback rules atomically with workflow runs?
- Secrets/credential management for broker/OMS connectors on free-tier infra (Workers + Supabase) without paid vaults?
- Data vendor connector licensing and rate limits: which vendors are allowed on OSS/free infra and what throttling model is required?
- Audit log retention and immutability guarantees: append-only storage choice and legal hold requirements?
