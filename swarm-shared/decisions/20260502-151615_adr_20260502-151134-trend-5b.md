## Deployment
cf workers

## v0.1 scope
v0.1 in 2 weeks: single deterministic pipeline (research snapshot → signal → risk guardrails → simulated execution) with Redis Streams orchestration, Postgres audit trail, replay endpoint, and one mock broker adapter; edge worker for auth and fan-out; latency measurement and basic dashboard/logs; no live broker connectivity yet.

## Open questions
- Which brokers/data vendors will be supported first and what auth/protocols (FIX, REST, WebSocket) are required?
- What deterministic replay mechanism is needed (event sourcing vs snapshots) to meet compliance forensics?
- What are the hard risk guardrail thresholds (per trade/per account/per venue) and how will they be updated safely?
- Target latency SLO breakdown by stage (research→signal, signal→risk, risk→execution) to achieve <500ms median?
- How will secrets/credentials be managed in CF Workers vs local dev while remaining free-tier friendly?
- What observability stack (logs/traces/metrics) will be used to measure latency and detect regressions on free infra?
