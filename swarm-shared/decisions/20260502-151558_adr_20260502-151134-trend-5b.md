## v0.1 scope
v0.1 in 2 weeks: one strategy (mean-reversion on single symbol), research→signal→risk→execution pipeline with Redis Streams orchestrator, Supabase EventLog for replay, mock broker adapter, risk rule (max notional), latency dashboard and one-click replay; median signal-to-execution <500ms on free-tier infra (CF Workers + Supabase + Redis).

## Open questions
- Broker/data-vendor connector latency SLOs and auth scopes (paper vs live, FIX vs REST/WebSocket)?
- Deterministic replay storage budget and retention policy for compliance (how many months of EventLog on free tier)?
- Cold-start latency targets for CF Workers vs HF Space; which runtime yields <500ms p95 end-to-end on free tier?
- Risk engine rules: static thresholds vs dynamic ML guardrails; what is acceptable false-positive rate for v0.1?
- Idempotency and exactly-once semantics across Redis Streams and Postgres: dedupe strategy and at-least-once vs exactly-once trade-offs?
