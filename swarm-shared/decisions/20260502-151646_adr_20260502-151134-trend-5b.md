## v0.1 scope
v0.1 in 2 weeks: single deterministic workflow template (research->signal->risk check->execution stub) with Postgres-backed event store, Redis-backed queue and cache, compliance guardrail evaluator (JSON Logic), idempotent execution adapter interface (mock broker), immutable audit entries, and replay endpoint to re-run a workflow_run deterministically from its context_snapshot.

## Open questions
- Which brokers/OMS/EMS and data vendors must be prioritized for v0.1 connectors?
- What deterministic replay fidelity is required (event-level vs state snapshot) and retention period?
- Which compliance rule DSL (JSON Logic vs custom) balances expressiveness and audit simplicity?
- What latency SLA (p50/p99) from signal to execution attempt is acceptable on free-tier infra?
- How will secrets/credentials for broker adapters be provisioned and rotated on free-tier hosts?
