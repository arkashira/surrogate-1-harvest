## Deployment
cf workers

## v0.1 scope
v0.1 in 2 weeks: single deterministic workflow (research→signal→risk→execution) with replayable runs, Postgres audit trail, Redis risk cache, one broker adapter (sandbox), Cloudflare Queues for orchestration, and immutable hash-chain ledger; supports manual trigger and one compliance guardrail (max position limit).

## Open questions
- Determinism boundaries: which steps must be pure functions vs. allowed nondeterministic I/O, and how to snapshot inputs for replay?
- Latency SLA per hop (research→signal→risk→execution) and acceptable queue depth under bursty market data
- Compliance rule hot-reload semantics (can rules change mid-run?) and versioning strategy for audits
- Broker/OMS idempotency key scheme and reconciliation cadence for ledger consistency
- Secrets/credential custody for broker keys (CF Workers KV vs. Supabase Vault vs. local envelope encryption)
